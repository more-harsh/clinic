About Us

Every human being is exclusive and so is their personality. Facial expressions contribute immensely to a charming and welcoming personality. A clean, hygienic and dentition make these expressions and for such an exclusive dentition it is necessary that unique, innovative and exclusive treatment planning and execution is required. Align Dental Studio with the motto of, “As Distinctive As You Are”, aims to offer quality and exclusivity to every patient. It requires an exclusive solution to every dental problem.

Align Dental Studio is headed by Dr. Devikka Minase, A Gold Medalist, and a team of 5 Specialists. Dr. Devikka Minase and her team have cumulative experience of 30+years.
